Brief Pangong Lake Overview

Pangong Lake is one of Ladakh's most iconic destinations, known for its stunning blue waters and dramatic Himalayan backdrop. Travel with us for a smooth, well-paced trip that highlights the lake's natural beauty and nearby high-altitude attractions.

Lakeside Beauty

Enjoy sunrise and sunset over Pangong's shimmering waters framed by crisp mountain peaks.

Mountain Adventure

Pass through scenic high-altitude routes with stops at Chang La, Khardung La, and local view points.

Peaceful Escape

Relax in comfortable tented camps by the lake with warm hospitality and calm evening skies.

Suggested Ladakh Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Leh

Arrive in Leh and transfer to your hotel. Acclimatize gently with a relaxed evening and explore the local market.

Day 2: Leh to Pangong Lake

Drive to Pangong Lake via Chang La pass, enjoy the lake view, and stay overnight in a lakeside camp with sunset panorama.

Day 3: Return to Leh

Spend the morning at the lake, then return to Leh for a cultural walk and preparation for departure.

Note: The itinerary can be extended with additional nights, Nubra Valley tours, monastery visits, or a relaxed stay in Leh for full acclimatization.
