Kaziranga National Park Tour

Discover the wild heart of Assam with Ghumo Duniya. Kaziranga National Park is home to the one-horned rhinoceros, rich birdlife, and serene river landscapes—all packaged into a memorable wildlife journey.

Create Your Kaziranga Safari

Brief Kaziranga Travel Overview

Kaziranga offers a unique blend of wildlife adventure and nature immersion. With expert guides, comfortable lodging, and scenic safaris, this package is ideal for travelers who want an unforgettable national park experience.

Wildlife Safari

Enjoy guided jeep and elephant safaris to spot rhinos, elephants, tigers, and rare bird species.

Nature Discovery

Explore lush grasslands, the Brahmaputra riverbanks, and habitats protected by UNESCO.

Comfort Stay

Relax in comfortable eco-resorts and lodges with traditional Assamese hospitality.

Suggested Kaziranga Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati

Arrive in Guwahati and transfer to your Kaziranga lodge. Enjoy a welcome briefing and evening nature walk.

Day 2: Morning Elephant Safari

Begin with an early morning elephant safari through the national park, followed by a jeep safari in the afternoon.

Day 3: Birdwatching and Departure

Enjoy a guided birdwatching walk, visit local tea gardens, and depart after lunch or continue to your next destination.

Note: This itinerary can be extended with additional days, river cruises, cultural tours, or a visit to nearby tea estates.
