Brief Jodhpur Travel Overview

Jodhpur is a striking destination of royal forts, bluewashed lanes, and vibrant markets. Enjoy a curated tour that highlights Mehrangarh Fort, local bazaars, and authentic Rajasthani hospitality.

Mehrangarh Fort

Explore the fort's palaces, museums, and sweeping city views from one of Rajasthan's most iconic landmarks.

Blue City Streets

Walk through the charming blue lanes of the old city, filled with artisan shops, spice vendors, and historic courtyards.

Local Culture

Experience traditional cuisine, folk music, and the warm hospitality of Jodhpur's heritage hotels.

Suggested Jodhpur Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & City Orientation

Arrive in Jodhpur, check into your hotel, and enjoy a relaxed orientation walk through the blue city, with welcome tea and local stories.

Day 2: Mehrangarh Fort & Local Markets

Visit Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and the bustling Sardar Market with a guided tour of the fort's galleries and courtyards.

Day 3: Culture & Departure

Discover Bishnoi villages or explore local handloom and handicraft centers before a smooth transfer to the airport or train station.

Note: This itinerary can be extended with a desert camp experience, Mandore Gardens visit, or a luxury palace stay in Jodhpur.
