Brief Agra Travel Overview

Agra is home to the world-renowned Taj Mahal and stands as a testament to India's architectural brilliance. Your trip includes the iconic monument, Agra Fort, local markets, and the best travel comfort with Ghumo Duniya's expert planning.

Taj Mahal Wonder

Witness the ethereal beauty of the Taj Mahal with sunrise and sunset visits, guided tours, and photography moments.

Mughal Heritage

Explore Agra Fort, Itimad-ud-Daulah, and local bazaars to feel the richness of Mughal history and culture.

Comfort & Convenience

Stay in well-located hotels, enjoy comfortable transfers, and travel with professionally guided heritage tours.

Suggested Agra Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Agra

Arrive in Agra, transfer to your hotel, and enjoy an evening at the Taj Mahal with sunset views and guided orientation.

Day 2: Taj Mahal & Agra Heritage

Start with sunrise at the Taj Mahal, then explore Agra Fort, Itimad-ud-Daulah, and local markets with expert guides.

Day 3: Relaxation & Departure

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast, optional local market walk, and smooth airport transfer or connection to your next destination.

Note: This is a flexible itinerary. Ghumo Duniya can extend it with Mathura-Vrindavan excursions, luxury heritage stays, or Delhi combination tours.
